Cafe Culture

Cafes are the heart and soul of the Seoul. They add a relaxing feel to the city vibes. The cafe ranges from fun manga cafes to VR cafes. The dishes often ranges from cordon bleu level pastries to home made pastries with love. Cafes for toddlers babychino and for tired parents. There are seldom cafes that are family friendly. SeoulMate's Top Pick: matcha tea in matchacha,seongondong Visit them

Restaurants

Cuisine and comfort dining. Seoul is home to many different restaurants ranging from the vast multitude of cuisine they are perfect for formal settings and company meetings. The alcohol served is high end and often imported. SeoulMate's Top Pick: Ossuserimoshi 30 Baekjegobun-ro 45-gil, Songpa 1(il)-dong, Songpa-gu, Seoul, South Korea.

Street Food

While there’s a tantalizing restaurant every few meters throughout the entire city, it’s street food carts and tents that fill all the open spaces. Walking through Seoul is like having to walk through a slot canyon of culinary goodies all calling your name.
